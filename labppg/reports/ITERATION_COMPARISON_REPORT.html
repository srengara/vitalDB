8. Conclusion

Impact of Skipping Single-Peak Check

  • Success rate: 62 → 66 samples (+4 change, 13 recovered, 9 regressed)
  • MAE: 31.1 → 37.9 mg/dL (+6.8)
  • Zone A: 53.2% → 40.9% (-12.3pp)

Overall verdict: Skipping the single-peak check worsened accuracy. While it recovered 13 previously-failed samples, the additional multi-peak windows added noise that degraded predictions on average. The single-peak filter acts as a quality gate that, despite reducing window count, ensures cleaner PPG morphology for prediction.
